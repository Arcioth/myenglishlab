Getting Started Guide

Follow these steps to start solving.

1. Installation

Download the extension and pin it to your browser toolbar for easy access.

2. Login

Open the extension popup and log in with the credentials provided in your welcome email.

3. Usage

Navigate to a MyEnglishLab exercise. Open the popup and click "Solve Page". Correct answers will be filled automatically.

4. Auto Mode

For multiple pages, toggle "Full Auto". The extension will solve, submit, and move to the next page automatically.
